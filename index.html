Key Features

  • Group and manage routines with batch notifications
  • Simple and intuitive operation
  • Clear and user-friendly UI

How to Use

Basic Usage

  1. Create a group: Tap the "+" button in the top right
  2. Add an alarm: Tap the "+" button within a group
  3. Set time: Tap the clock icon to select time
  4. Manage notifications: Swipe to edit or delete

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What if the alarm doesn't sound?

A: Please check if notifications are enabled in your device settings.

Q: How do notifications work with Apple Watch?

A: Notifications are automatically sent to your paired Apple Watch.

Q: How do I manage groups?

A: Swipe left to edit, swipe right to delete groups. You can also toggle groups on/off in batch.
