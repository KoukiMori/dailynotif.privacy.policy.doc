Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Daily Notif (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Alarm settings (time, title)
  • App usage data (crash reports, performance data)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing alarm functionality
  • Improving the App and fixing bugs
  • Providing user support

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • With your consent
  • When necessary for App improvement (as anonymized statistical data)

5. Data Storage

Alarm settings are stored only on your device and are not stored in the cloud.

6. Permissions Used

The App uses the following permissions:

  • Notification permission: For sending alarm notifications
