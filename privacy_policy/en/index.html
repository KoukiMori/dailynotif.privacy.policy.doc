Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Daily Notif (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects user privacy and strives to protect personal information. This Privacy Policy explains the information collected and how it is used when using the App.

2. Information Collected

The App collects the following information:

  • Alarm settings (time, title)
  • App usage data (crash reports, performance data)

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing alarm functionality
  • App improvement and bug fixes
  • Providing user support

4. Information Sharing

The App does not share collected information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • When user consent is obtained
  • When necessary for app improvement (as anonymized statistical data)

5. Data Storage

Alarm settings are stored only on the device and are not saved to the cloud.

6. Permission Usage

The App uses the following permissions:

  • Notification permission: For sending alarm notifications
